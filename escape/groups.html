Choose the perfect escape room for your group
With 3 current rooms and more coming soon, Ice Break Escape Rooms are intertwined with complex narratives and all kinds of high-tech and clue-based puzzles. From our witchcraft themed Silent room to our adventure-based X-Case room and the suspenseful, dramatic Vanishing room, you'll find the perfect escape room for large groups of all kinds.
  • SILENT ROOM
    • expand_more expand_less

    This is the ideal escape room for 5 players who are escape room novices or simply prefer to experience escape rooms with lower challenge levels. The silent room will see you undertaking clue-based challenges suited to groups aged 14 years old and above in a witchcraft-themed environment.

  • X-CASE ROOM
    • expand_more expand_less

    The X-Case room is a little different from the usual 6-player escape room in that it requires some physical climbing and problem-solving skills. If you're looking for a serious challenge while also solving a complicated murder mystery, this room is the perfect task to tackle with your friends.

  • VANISHING ROOM
    • expand_more expand_less

    One of the harder rooms to master, our vanishing room will see you completing puzzles to solve a mysterious disappearance. It is an ideal escape room for 10 players or more because, in this room, the more brain power you have on your side, the better!
