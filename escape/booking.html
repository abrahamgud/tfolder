Booking Instructions

close
  1. Choose the number of players.
  2. Select a game from the drop menu.
  3. Pick the date and time for the booking (please CHECK THIS CAREFULLY before confirming).
  4. Follow the prompts and enter your details and payment information.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email. If you want to make changes, there is a 'View/Change Booking' link in the email. If you have any issues, please call or email us.
  6. FOR LARGE GROUPS: If you have a large group (exceeding 10 players) or would like to split into groups to play our 2 games at the same time, please contact us through phone or email. We will do our best to move sessions around to suit you!
