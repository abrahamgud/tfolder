The story behind the Silent Room

A long time ago, there was a famous fortune teller who lived in an old log cabin. If you wanted him to answer your questions and tell you what the future had in store for you, all you had to do was pay him in gold coins. However, one day, out of the blue, the fortune teller and the log cabin became deathly quiet. Never uttering a sound again, the fortune teller became known as Mr. Silent.


A few years later, you find yourself standing in front of the old, silent log cabin...
