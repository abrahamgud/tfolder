We don't have scary theme or jump scares. You will find some dark rooms and props, yet we can assure you that no scary or jump scare content whatsoever. They only exist for creating atmosphere or a fluent storyline.

Perfect for team building! Just remember team work is the top tip for our rooms. Your team will have an absolutely remarkable escape experience.

How many people can play? expand_more expand_less

We can have maximum 21 people in three rooms.

If you have a large group that want to start at the same time, please contact us before making a booking.

Does it have any limit? expand_more expand_less

You must be 14 years or over to play our rooms. Younger players must be accompanied by adult.

Booking Assist

How to make a booking? expand_more expand_less

All bookings for escape room can be made via our online BOOKING page. We require people to book ahead, as we cannot guarantee walk-in availability.

Can we have more players than booked? expand_more expand_less

Yes you can! If you have more people, as long as you don't exceed the maximum number of people permitted for each room, you can just pay the difference whn you arrive.

Can we have less players than booked? expand_more expand_less

Yes you can! If you have less people, simply use change booking link in your conformation email to make changes. Please note, if this change is made within 24hrs, 50% of fee for not coming players will be charged due to the nature of the game.

Can we do 2 rooms at the same time? expand_more expand_less

Of course you can! If you have a large group (exceeding the limit for different theme) and want to split into groups to play our games at the same time, please call or email us. We would do our best to move the sessions to suit you!

More questions about Booking? expand_more expand_less

Information for Player

How long is the game? expand_more expand_less

There is a 15 mins introduction, the game itself takes 60 mins. We will also have a short debrief if you still want to know more about the game. A group photo can also be taken afterwards. Total takes about 90mins.

Please arrive 15 mins before your booking time for the introduction.

What do I need to bring? expand_more expand_less

Just bring your wisdom and eyes of observation when you go into the room!

If you have some personal belongings, we will provide you a locker before the game, and you will have the key with you during the game.

Will I really be locked in? expand_more expand_less

No, our staff wil unlock the door in the first place if you tell us you feel uncomfortable being locked in the room.

Just note: If you leave the game, the countdown clock does not stop as we need to maintain our booking schedule.

What if I can't solve a clue? expand_more expand_less

You will take a walkie talkie with you during the whole game session. You can ask for clues if needed. We will also have a staff member monitoring you at all times via our cameras in the room. They know exactly what stage you are on and can assist you at any stage.

What if I am late for my booking? expand_more expand_less

If your team arrives late you will still be able to play. Unfortunately, we will not be able to give you a full 60 minutes to escape. This is because we need time to get the room ready for the next booking.

What if I am unable to escape? expand_more expand_less

You are locked in forever. Just kidding! Your Game Master will let you out and explain any unsolved puzzles to you if you want to know.
