About Us

Ice Break Escape Room has created rooms which emphasis puzzle logic, striving to let players immerse themselves in the environment. We value the importance of teamwork for players and have ensured every player can be involved in the game. Our escape rooms are intertwined with thoughtful narrative and a variety of logic, dexterity, high-tech and clue based puzzles
